Needfinding Design Prototype Team Challenges

Need Finding

The needfinding process included a survey of several participants on their organizational habits and their use of AI assistants. The survey was conducted to understand the user's needs and preferences. It was conducted on a sample of 10 participants. To the left is some results of our survey.

Design

The design process was focused on extreme simplicity, as the goal is to reduce the number of taps the user needed to organize their life via AI. However, we also wanted to be very clear as to what our value add was, so we created the designs to the left. When logged in, you can simply add connections to popular note taking apps.

Prototype

The prototype established the feasibility of a computer vision based assistant, showing it could generate relevant tasks based on the user's screen. It also helped us recognize many techinical challenges, which will be discussed below.

Team

Our team has a variety of talents and abilities allowing us to create this product well. See more on the left.

Challenges

The prototype was a great learning experience, but it also showed us many challenges. The biggest challenge was the latency in response time of the AI agent. We attempted to design our architecture to avoid this network + processing lag, but it is extraordinarily difficult to do so. We also had challenges with the accuracy of the computer vision model, which would often misinterpret the user's screen, giving them irrelevant tasks.
